About Masters Janitorial Services

We are Cleaning Company
since 2015

Established in May 2015 under The Companies Act 2019 (ACT 992), Masters Janitorial Services transforms spaces with eco-friendly cleaning solutions. We deliver sustainable excellence across commercial and residential properties, creating clean environments that inspire success. When you choose MJS, your establishment benefits from:

  • Consistently sanitized restrooms

  • Pristine, clear glass surfaces

  • Competitive pricing without compromising quality
